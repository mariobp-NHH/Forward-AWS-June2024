1. Choose Your Vehicle

Select the type of vehicle you use for commuting to work.

2. Enter Your Daily Travel Distance

Enter the total kilometers you travel per day (including both ways), Choose the type of fuel or energy your vehicle uses and indicate the number of days per week you use this vehicle for commuting.

3. Repeat for Other Vehicles

If you use multiple vehicles during the week, repeat steps 2 to 4 for each one. The total number of days can't exceed 7!

4. View Your Data

After saving your entries, you can review them anytime in the “Your Data” section, where you'll see all registered trips along with their calculated CO₂ emissions.
