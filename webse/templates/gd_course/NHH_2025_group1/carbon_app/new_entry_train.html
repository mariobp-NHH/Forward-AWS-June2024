Our Methodology

Train travel in the Nordic countries is highly eco-friendly, emitting 13 times less CO₂ than diesel and 3 times less than the European average, thanks to cleaner energy sources. Norway has an extensive rail network, including connections across Europe. However, diesel trains are still used north of Mjøsa due to non-electrified lines. (View our report here)

Using the formula:
\(CO₂ = d ⋅ ef,\) and lightrail emission being 0.091kg/km for diesel,
0.007kg/km for Nordic,
0.026kg/km for Europe
