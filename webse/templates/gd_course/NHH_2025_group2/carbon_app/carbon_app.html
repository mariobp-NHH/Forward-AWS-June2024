Carbon App Calculator

To get started, simply chose your transport method and enter your travel distance. Our app will provide both a visual and numerical breakdown of your travel emissions. You can filter your entries by date and choose whether to view only your own entries or all entries from your organization. If you need to delete an entry, scroll down to the table at the bottom of the page and click the 'Delete' button beside the entry you wish to remove. The table also allows you to filter your entries by for example date, number of kilometers, or mode of transport. Additionally, you can use the search bar to find a specific entry.
