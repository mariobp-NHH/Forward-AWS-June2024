Our Purpose

We've created this app to make it easy for you to measure and understand your carbon footprint. With a user-friendly interface, our app gives you a clear picture of your carbon emissions based on your personal input. By using the Carbon App Calculator, you'll discover what factors affect your carbon footprint and learn practical steps you can take to reduce it. We hope our app helps you make more sustainable choices and supports your journey towards reducing your environmental impact.



Methodology

Interested in learning more about how our app works behind the scenes? Head over to our Methodology page, where you can find detailed information on the data sources and calculation methods we use.
