METHODOLOGY

How we estimate transport emissions

This calculator estimates transport emissions with transparent formulas and fixed emission factors.
The result is an approximation designed to compare transport choices.

Paper

Choose methodology type

Switch between individual and business methodology to view the relevant formulas and emission factors.

Public transport

CO2 = Distance × Emission factor

Distance is entered in kilometers. Emission factors are expressed either as g/km or g/passenger-km depending on transport mode.

Private transport

CO2 = Distance × Emission factor Passengers

For private modes, emissions are allocated per passenger.

Business transport

CO2 = Distance × Emission factor

Used for business transport modes measured in g/km, such as vans.

Freight transport

CO2 = Distance × Weight × Emission factor

Used for freight modes measured in g/tonne-km, such as trucks, rail freight, cargo planes, maritime transport, and pipelines.
