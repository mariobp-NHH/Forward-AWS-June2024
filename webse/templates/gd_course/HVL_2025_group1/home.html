Did you know...

Norway must reduce its carbon emission by 55% by 2030. To achieve this YOU have to lower your emission by 3,6 ton spread over five years from now. That means a maximum emission of 720 kg per year, and 14kg each week. Our calculator can be used for “what-if planning”, helping you explore which transport choices lead to the lowest emissions. This way you can help reduce Norway's emission by 2030 with 55%.
